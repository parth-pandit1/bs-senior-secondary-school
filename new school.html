Principal’s Message

Welcome to M.J. Convent School. Education is not just about academic success but about shaping character and values. Academics

Our academic structure is carefully designed to meet the developmental needs of students at every stage.

Pre-Primary

Play-based learning focusing on language development, numbers, creativity, and social skills in a joyful environment.

Primary

Strong foundation in core subjects with emphasis on reading, writing, mathematics, and activity-based learning.

Secondary & Senior Secondary

Concept-driven teaching, exam preparation, career guidance, and overall personality development.

Rules & Discipline

Students must wear the prescribed school uniform with pride.
